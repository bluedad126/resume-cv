## CURRENT RESEARCH

### Adversarial Prompt Experiment Log | April 2025–Present
Independent research examining LLM vulnerabilities through adversarial poetry and prompt engineering. Documenting organic instances of model misalignment (hallucinations, personification, scope creep) and conducting systematic experiments targeting specific failure modes. Work includes successful single-turn jailbreak attempts and analysis of language-based attack vectors. Research log forthcoming on GitHub.

### Critical Internet Research: The Non/Othered Girl in the Age of AI | November 2024–Present
Long-form essay series examining otherness, supremacy, and identity construction in digital modernity, with focus on weaponized girlhood (Black girls, queer/effeminate boys, disabled girls, refugee girls, abused girls). Building theoretical bridges between classical theses on selfhood and contemporary AI alignment challenges. Essays published on Notion.
